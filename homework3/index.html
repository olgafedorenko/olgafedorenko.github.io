ABC Toys

Making learning fun

ABC Toys is a company that opened its doors in 2001 and it has kept its mission for all those years.

The mission of our company is to create toys that are safe for kindergarten kids but that challenge the kids' mind and motor skills making learning a fun task.

By the way, parents love our toys too

Our toys

Melissa Doug Shape Sorting Cube - Classic Wooden Toy With 12 Shapes

This ultimate shape sorter features 12 chunky, vibrantly colored shapes that make a satisfying "clunk" as they drop into the natural-finish hardwood cube. Then open the lid, take them out and start all over again! A classic educational toy for toddlers.

Melissa Doug Deluxe Pounding Bench Wooden Toy With Mallet

The pegs on the bench go up and down, playing peekaboo on this solid wood pounding activity. Non-removable pegs take turns showing their smiles while your toddler has fun naming the colors and enjoys practicing fine motor skills. A sturdy mallet is included.

Melissa Doug Solid Wood Project Workbench Play Building Set

Keep that little builder busy with this sturdy wooden workbench that's just the right size. Right down to the nuts and bolts, this set includes a functioning vise, tool rack, wooden tools and hardware and a storage shelf. Ideas to spark the imagination are included!

Wooden Cutting and Cooking Pretend Play Set, Stir, Serve Utensils, Pots and Pans, Learning Educational Kitchen Toy

Children will gain fine motor skills and grip skills by cutting, numeracy skills by counting the pieces,Assist children with understanding fractions as they cut and naming colors. Durable, safe toy. Great fun highest quality materials made, encourages children to utilize creativity and role-play. Includes cookware, basic food and utensils.

Plan Toy Tea Set(Solid Wood Version)

The tea set consist of a teapot, a sugar bowl, a milk pitcher, 2 tea cups, 2 saucers, 2 tea spoons, 2 tea bags and 2 pieces of sugar. PlanToys creates toys that inspire children's imagination as well as promote their physical and intellectual development. Our uncompromising quality is accomplished by the work of our management team; our experienced designers; and our manufacturing, packaging and shipping personnel.
